Personal Presentation

My name is Espen. I am born in 1964 and Norwegian. This is the way I could start. Then you would know all there is to know. Who I am - what to suspect, what to believe, or not to believe when I open my mouth...

Or I could start with my education. But what does my years studying Mathematics, ICT, Spanish, Media, journalism and Multimedia technology have to do with it?

Or my work... yes I have been working in many branches, gathering experiences with tourism, building industry, taxi driving and much more. Also running my own company, I have.

But I'll start with my interests - drawing and video, animation and interaction, programming and electronics. Different kind of sports, history, Permaculture and positive activism.

But over all, the search for truth and the expression of the 'impossible' - revealing the fascinating complexity of history, politics and spirit, the madness of our worlds' self destructing forces... expressing it all in a holistic and multi-dimensional format through art, technology and spirit.

Like an Onion is the Truth... Many layers and ... Tears.
My studies may give new insights - and looking for insights I am.
